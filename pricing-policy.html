Pricing &
Payment Policy

Everything you need to know about how we structure payments, handle deposits, and manage project milestones — before we get started.

Last updated: May 2026

01

Deposit Requirement

White Space Designs requires a non-refundable deposit before any work commences on a project. This applies to all web design, development, and AI agent engagements, regardless of project size or client location.

No deposit = no start date. Your project slot is only confirmed and reserved once the deposit payment has been received and cleared.

The deposit covers the initial discovery, research, and design phase of your project. It also protects both parties — you get a committed timeline, we can allocate the right resources without risk.

02

Payment Structure by Service

Payment is split across project milestones. The structure varies by service type:

Service Deposit (upfront) Balance
Web Design & Development 50% before work starts 50% before final delivery & handoff
AI Agent Development 50% before build starts 50% before deployment & access transfer
Brand Identity / UI UX 50% before work starts 50% on final file delivery
Retainer Plans (Starter / Growth / Pro) First full month upfront Monthly thereafter (billed on same date)
Rush / Expedited Projects 100% upfront —

The balance is due before the final deliverable is handed over. No assets, credentials, source files, or live deployments are transferred until the account is settled in full.

03

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • South African clients: EFT / bank transfer (ZAR). Banking details are provided on the invoice.
  • International clients: Wise (TransferWise), PayPal, or another mutually agreed platform. All international invoices are quoted in USD or ZAR at the agreed exchange rate on the date of quote.
  • Retainer billing: Monthly invoice sent 5 days before the billing date. Payment due within 3 business days.

All prices are quoted exclusive of VAT unless otherwise stated. White Space Designs reserves the right to adjust pricing for currency fluctuation on international projects where payment is delayed beyond 7 days from quote date.

04

Cancellation Policy

We understand plans change. Our cancellation terms are designed to be fair to both parties:

  • Before work commences: If you cancel after paying the deposit but before any design or development work has started, a 15% administration fee is deducted and the remainder is refunded within 5–7 business days.
  • After work commences: The deposit is non-refundable once discovery, research, or design work has begun. Any work completed up to the cancellation point remains the property of White Space Designs until the account is settled.
  • Retainer cancellations: Retainer agreements require 30 days written notice to cancel. No refunds are issued for the current billing period.
  • Client-caused delays: If a project stalls for more than 30 days due to lack of client feedback, content, or approvals, we reserve the right to invoice for work completed to date and release the project slot.
05

Revisions & Scope Changes

Each project includes a set number of revision rounds as agreed in your project brief. Revisions are defined as adjustments within the original scope — not new features or direction changes.

  • Revision rounds are outlined in the project proposal before work starts.
  • Requests that fall outside the original scope are quoted separately and billed as an addition.
  • Scope changes that significantly alter the project direction may require a revised deposit.
06

Project Handoff

Once the final balance is received, all deliverables are transferred within 2 business days. For clients who cannot be present in person, we provide a full remote handoff:

  • A recorded Loom walkthrough of the completed project, covering all key functionality and settings.
  • A PDF or Notion guide covering logins, how to make common updates, and who to contact for support.
  • An optional live video call (30 min) to walk through the handoff in real time — available at no extra charge for all web and AI projects.
  • Transfer of all credentials, source files, and access on the same day as the live call or within 24 hours of the recorded walkthrough being delivered.

International clients are accommodated across time zones — we schedule handoff calls to suit your region.

07

Late Payments

Invoices are due within the timeframe stated on the invoice (typically 3–5 business days). If payment is not received by the due date:

  • Work is paused after 5 business days overdue. No new deliverables will be sent until the account is settled.
  • A late payment fee of 5% of the outstanding balance is applied after 10 business days overdue.
  • For retainer clients, services (hosting, active agents, support) are suspended after 7 days without payment and resumed within 24 hours of receipt.
08

International Clients

White Space Designs works with clients across Southern Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and beyond. For international engagements:

  • All project quotes for international clients are issued in USD (or agreed currency) at the rate on the quote date.
  • Bank transfer fees and currency conversion costs are the responsibility of the client — please ensure the full invoiced amount is received net of fees.
  • Wise (TransferWise) is our preferred platform for international payments due to low fees and fast settlement.
  • All handoffs, calls, and communication are conducted in English via email, WhatsApp, or video call.
09

Website Maintenance Packages

For clients who already have a site and just need it kept running, secure, and up to date. Maintenance plans are separate from retainer plans — they cover your existing build, not new development.

Plan Price What's included
Basic R1,500 / month Hosting management, uptime monitoring, security updates, plugin/dependency updates
Standard R2,500 / month Everything in Basic + content updates (text, images, pages), weekly backups, monthly performance report
Premium R4,500 / month Everything in Standard + priority support (same-day response), performance optimisation, analytics review, minor redesign tweaks
Not sure which plan? If your site was built by White Space Designs, Standard is the default recommendation. Premium is for high-traffic or revenue-generating sites where downtime has a direct cost.

Maintenance plans are billed monthly, one month upfront. 30 days written notice required to cancel. Plans can be upgraded or downgraded at the start of any new billing cycle.
