About this property

Positioned on one of Aloe Ridge Estate's most private stands, this elegant double-storey residence offers the kind of measured luxury that improves with time. Designed to take full advantage of the north-facing aspect, the ground floor opens from a formal entrance foyer into a sequence of connected living spaces — an intimate sitting room, an open-plan kitchen and family room, and a generous entertaining terrace with motorised shade sails and a heated pool beyond.

The kitchen has been finished to a higher specification than is typical at this price point: slab-format porcelain counters, sub-zero appliance integration, and a scullery large enough to function as a second kitchen when the house is in full use. Upstairs, the master suite occupies the full east wing — walk-in dressing room, en-suite with freestanding bath, and a private balcony over the garden. Three further bedrooms share a Jack-and-Jill bathroom and a second family bathroom.

The property presents as new throughout. All major systems — air conditioning, underfloor heating, irrigation, and the solar-assisted geyser — were either replaced or installed in the 2022–2023 renovation. One of the garages has been converted to a home gym; this is easily reversed. The seller is open to including certain furniture.
