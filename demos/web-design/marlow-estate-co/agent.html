About Catherine

Catherine Marlow began her career in property in 1993, joining one of South Africa's largest residential franchises as a junior agent in Pretoria East. Within three years she was the branch's top performer. Over the decade that followed she worked across Tshwane and Ekurhuleni, developing a reputation for direct advice, meticulous preparation and a consistent ability to close in difficult market conditions.

She founded Marlow Estate Co. in 2003 with a deliberate counterpoint to the franchise model: fewer mandates, deeper relationships, and a refusal to accept listings priced above market in order to win business. That approach cost her in volume but built an exceptional reputation for achieving asking price or above — a track record she and the firm have maintained for more than two decades.

Catherine personally oversees every residential mandate valued above R4 million and all commercial transactions. She is a regular contributor to the Centurion Property Digest and sits on the board of the local branch of the Institute of Estate Agents. She is also a qualified property valuer and provides sworn appraisals for financial institutions.
