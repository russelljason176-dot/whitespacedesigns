Free property assessment

What is your
home worth?

A Marlow Estate Co. valuation is not an automated algorithm or a comparative spreadsheet. It is a considered, in-person assessment carried out by one of our senior agents — covering location premiums, condition, recent comparable sales and the particular character of your property that online tools will always miss.

  1. 1

    Submit your details

    Complete the form with your property address, a brief description, and your preferred contact time. We'll confirm receipt within one business hour.

  2. 2

    Initial desk research

    Before we visit, your assigned agent will review deeds office records, recent comparable sales in your micro-market, and current demand signals for your property type and area.

  3. 3

    On-site assessment

    A senior agent visits in person. The walk-through typically takes 30–45 minutes. We assess condition, positioning, unique features and any factors that affect value in either direction.

  4. 4

    Written valuation report

    You receive a written summary with our recommended asking range, comparable sales evidence, and an honest view on likely time to sale at different price points. No obligation to list with us.

This service is provided free of charge with no obligation. Marlow Estate Co. does not share your details with third parties. Our valuation constitutes an informed opinion of market value and does not constitute a formal sworn appraisal for banking or legal purposes. For sworn appraisals, contact us separately.
