  • Bond repayments are based on a flat amortising structure — principal plus interest, no balloon payments.

  • Your actual interest rate will depend on your credit profile, employment status, and the bank's assessment of the property. Bond origination through an approved bond originator (e.g. ooba, BetterBond) typically results in better rates than applying directly.
  • These figures are illustrative only and do not constitute financial advice. Speak to a qualified bond originator before committing to any purchase.
