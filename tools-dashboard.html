Google Maps API Key — go to console.cloud.google.com → Credentials → copy your key → paste into the Google Maps — Find Businesses node URL, replacing YOUR_GOOGLE_MAPS_API_KEY
2
Instagram Access Token — go to developers.facebook.com/tools/explorer → select your app → add permissions: instagram_basic, instagram_content_publish, pages_read_engagement → Generate Token → paste into all 3 Instagram nodes replacing YOUR_INSTAGRAM_ACCESS_TOKEN

4
Credential IDs — open each node in n8n and select from the dropdown: Groq nodes → Groq account · Sheets nodes → WSD new · Gmail nodes → Gmail account · Replicate node → Replicate API Key

Design

Glyphy Type
glyphy.io
Search, copy and paste special characters, glyphs, symbols, and unicode characters directly into your designs or code.
WSD use: Gold bullet points (✦ ◆ ▸), section dividers, decorative marks in landing page copy and email signatures. Makes premium layouts look intentional without images.
↗
Fockups Mockups
fockups.com
Free device mockup generator. Drop your screenshot into a phone, laptop, or tablet frame instantly. No Photoshop needed.
WSD use: Client deliverables and case studies. Show a client's new website inside a MacBook or iPhone — instantly looks more professional and justifies your pricing.
↗
Halftone Generator Texture
halftone.xoihazard.com
Turn any image into a halftone dot pattern. Generates SVG or PNG. Adjustable dot size, spacing, and angle.
WSD use: Background textures for hero sections and social posts. A halftone overlay on Replicate-generated images gives them a print/editorial feel that stands out in feeds.
↗
Symbol.wtf Symbols
symbol.wtf
Browse and copy thousands of unicode symbols, icons, and special characters. Better search than Glyphy for obscure marks.
WSD use: Good for arrows (→ ↗ ⟶), geometric shapes, and decorative marks. Pair with brand gold for section markers in proposals and slide decks.
↗
Tiny Tools Utilities
tinytools.design
Small, focused design utilities — contrast checkers, gradient generators, spacing tools. All free, no login.
WSD use: Quick contrast checks before client deliverables. Gradient tools for landing page backgrounds — faster than CSS by hand.
↗
Fontjoy Typography
fontjoy.com
AI-powered font pairing generator. Lock one font and generate complementary pairings instantly. All Google Fonts compatible.
WSD use: Lock Inter as body, generate display font options for headlines. Show clients 3 pairing options — they feel involved, you close faster. Note: tools.dia.tv is down — this replaces it.
↗
Spline 3D
spline.design
3D design tool that exports interactive scenes as web components. Your R4X robot hero runs on this.
WSD use: Hero sections and interactive brand elements. Free plan embeds are fine — no paid plan needed since we use the web component embed directly.
↗
Google Fonts Typography
fonts.google.com
Free web fonts. Your site uses Inter — same font Vercel, Linear, and most premium SaaS products use.
WSD use: Stick with Inter for body. For display fonts, try Bricolage Grotesque or Cabinet Grotesk — both luxury-adjacent and free.
↗
Coolors Colour
coolors.co
Generate and refine colour palettes. Lock your brand gold (#C8A96E) and generate complementary colours for client sites.
WSD use: Client onboarding — when a client can't describe their brand colours, run their logo through Coolors' image picker to extract them automatically.

Ideogram AI Images
ideogram.ai
AI image generator with the best text-in-image accuracy available. 4 variations per prompt. Free tier: 10 prompts/day (~40 images).
WSD use: Social posts with readable text overlays, logo concepts, hero mockups. Unlike Midjourney/DALL-E, text in images reads correctly. Quick social content when Replicate is overkill.

Microsoft Designer AI Design

Free AI-powered graphic design (Microsoft account required). Social posts, flyers, mood boards. Built-in DALL-E generation. Smart AI layout suggestions.
For your fashion designer girlfriend: Lookbooks, mood boards, Instagram content grids, trend boards, flat lays from text prompts. Describe a look, it builds the layout. Free, no watermarks.

M1M4 Mockups Mockups
m1m4.com
500+ apparel mockup templates for Canva. Drop designs onto t-shirts, hoodies, tracksuits, joggers, caps. No Photoshop — works in Canva.
For your fashion designer girlfriend: Show clients exactly what a design looks like on real clothing. Perfect for pitching collections or selling custom pieces. Free starter pack available.

LightPDF Utility
lightpdf.com
Online watermark remover for PDFs and images. AI detects and removes watermarks while preserving background. Free tier for small files.
WSD use: Removing watermarks from stock images or PDFs when sourcing reference material. Full PDF suite too (merge, compress, convert) — useful for client proposals. $49.99/yr for full access.
↗
GSAP + ScrollTrigger Animation
gsap.com
Industry-standard JS animation library. ScrollTrigger makes elements animate as users scroll. Used by Stripe, Linear, Vercel.
WSD use: Scroll animations on service cards, stats, and sections. Free for most uses. One CDN script tag — no install. This is the answer to "should I add animations to the site."
↗
Squoosh Performance
squoosh.app
Google's image compression tool. Converts to WebP and compresses without visible quality loss. In-browser, no upload to third parties.
WSD use: Every image before it goes into a client site. A 2MB PNG compressed to 80KB WebP is the difference between a 90 and 45 on PageSpeed. Directly affects SEO rankings.
↗
Replicate AI Images
replicate.com
Run AI models via API. Powers your Instagram image generation with SDXL. Pay per run — no monthly subscription needed.
WSD use: SDXL costs ~$0.0023 per image. 30 posts/month = under $0.10. Your token: saved in Credentials.md. Watch usage at replicate.com/account.
↗
Groq Console AI / LLM
console.groq.com
Where you manage your Groq API key. Groq runs llama3 at 800 tokens/second — fast and cheap for content and email writing nodes.
WSD use: Check usage and regenerate your API key here if it ever stops working. Free tier is generous — monitor it as your workflow scales.

n8n Automation
localhost:5678 (local)
Your automation backbone. All 5 marketing agents run here. Self-hosted on your Windows machine.
WSD use: Keep it running in the background. Kill Ollama and ChatGPT before starting to prevent RAM crashes. Export workflows before deleting anything — no cloud backup.

HyperFrames Video
hyperframes.co
Programmatic video composition. Describe a scene, add brand colours and fonts, render video content — no Premiere or After Effects.
WSD use: Short animated brand intros or social clips as part of client onboarding packages, generated from their brand guidelines. A real differentiator at your price point.

Use Claude Code for all real client builds. It writes directly to files, knows your brand guidelines, and keeps context across sessions. Artifacts is good for quick mockups only — can't save files.
