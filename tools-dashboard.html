Design Tools

Glyphy Type
glyphy.io
Search, copy and paste special characters, glyphs, symbols, and unicode characters directly into your designs or code.
WSD use: Gold bullet points (✦ ◆ ▸), section dividers, decorative marks in landing page copy and email signatures. Makes premium layouts look intentional without images.
↗
Fockups Mockups
fockups.com
Free device mockup generator. Drop your screenshot into a phone, laptop, or tablet frame instantly. No Photoshop needed.
WSD use: Client deliverables and case studies. Show a client's new website inside a MacBook or iPhone instead of a flat screenshot — instantly looks more professional and justifies your pricing.
↗
Halftone Generator Texture
halftone.xoihazard.com
Turn any image into a halftone dot pattern. Generates SVG or PNG. Adjustable dot size, spacing, and angle.
WSD use: Background textures for hero sections and social media posts. A halftone overlay on your Replicate-generated images gives them a print/editorial feel that stands out in Instagram feeds.
↗
Symbol.wtf Symbols
symbol.wtf
Browse and copy thousands of unicode symbols, icons, and special characters. Better search than Glyphy for finding obscure marks.
WSD use: Use alongside Glyphy. Good for finding arrows (→ ↗ ⟶), geometric shapes, and decorative marks. Pair with your brand gold colour for section markers in proposals and slide decks.
↗
Tiny Tools Utilities
tinytools.design
Collection of small, focused design utilities — contrast checkers, gradient generators, spacing tools, and more. All free, no login.
WSD use: Quick contrast checks before sending client deliverables. Also use the gradient tools when building landing page backgrounds — saves time vs doing it manually in CSS.
↗
Fontjoy Typography
fontjoy.com
AI-powered font pairing generator. Lock one font and generate complementary pairings instantly. All Google Fonts compatible.
WSD use: Client site typography. Lock Inter as body, generate display font options for headlines. Show clients 3 pairing options — they feel involved in the process, you close faster.

⚠ tools.dia.tv is currently down — replaced with Fontjoy. Update this card when you remember the correct URL.

Spline 3D
spline.design
3D design tool that exports interactive scenes as web components. Your R4X robot hero runs on this.
WSD use: Hero sections and interactive brand elements. Free plan embeds are fine. Paid plan needed for CSS export — not required since we use the web component embed directly.

GSAP + ScrollTrigger Animation
gsap.com
Industry-standard JavaScript animation library. ScrollTrigger plugin makes elements animate as the user scrolls — used by Stripe, Linear, Vercel.
WSD use: Add to index.html for scroll animations on service cards, stats, and sections. Free for most uses. One CDN script tag — no install needed. This is the answer to your "should I add animations" question.
↗
Replicate AI Images
replicate.com
Run AI models via API. Powers your Instagram image generation with SDXL. Pay per run — no monthly subscription.
WSD use: SDXL costs ~$0.0023 per image. 30 posts/month = under $0.10. Your token: already saved in Credentials.md. Watch your usage at replicate.com/account.
↗
Groq Console AI
console.groq.com
Where you manage your Groq API key. Groq runs llama3 at 800 tokens/second — fast and cheap for your content and email writing nodes.
WSD use: Check usage and regenerate your API key here if it ever stops working. Free tier is generous — monitor it as your workflow scales.

Google Fonts Typography
fonts.google.com
Free web fonts. Your site currently uses Inter — the same font Vercel, Linear, and most premium SaaS products use.
WSD use: Stick with Inter for body copy. If you want a display font for headlines, try Bricolage Grotesque or Cabinet Grotesk — both luxury-adjacent and free.

Coolors Colour
coolors.co
Generate and refine colour palettes. Lock your brand gold (#C8A96E) and generate complementary colours when building client sites.
WSD use: Client onboarding — when a client can't describe their brand colours, run their logo through Coolors' image picker to extract them automatically.

Squoosh Performance
squoosh.app
Google's image compression tool. Converts to WebP and compresses without visible quality loss.
WSD use: Every image before it goes into a client site. A 2MB PNG compressed to 80KB WebP is the difference between a 90 and 45 on Google PageSpeed. Directly affects SEO rankings.

Workflow Decisions

Site Building — Claude Code (me) vs Claude Artifacts

Use Claude Code (this session) for all real client site builds. I write directly to your files, remember your project context, know your brand guidelines, and can run the server to test it live.

Claude Artifacts (claude.ai) is good for quick mockups and experimenting with layouts — but it can't save files, doesn't know your codebase, and loses context between sessions.

✓ Use Claude Code for all client builds

Animations on Your Site Pages

Yes — 100%. A static site in 2026 reads as unfinished, especially at your price point. Clients buying a R7,500 website expect premium presentation from the person selling it.

The right approach: GSAP ScrollTrigger on index.html. Service cards, stats, and process steps slide/fade in as you scroll. One script tag, no framework. Same library Stripe and Linear use. Takes about an hour to add properly.
